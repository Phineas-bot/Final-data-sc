# Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize retention incentives for high-volume, high-frequency users.
   - These customers are the most valuable and are most likely to stay engaged with targeted rewards.

2. Develop merchant or payment bundles for users who demonstrate frequent transaction behavior.
   - Payment-heavy customers can be offered loyalty programs, cashback, or merchant-specific offers.

3. Monitor failed transaction attempts as a signal of user friction.
   - High failure rates may indicate usability issues, network problems, or blocked payments.

4. Use weekend activity trends to design promotions for off-peak or after-hours service adoption.
   - Medium and high activity users are already active on weekends, so weekend offers may deepen engagement.

5. Segment low activity users separately for educational outreach.
   - These users may benefit from simple guidance on how to use mobile money services effectively.
