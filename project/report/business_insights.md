# Business Insights and Recommendations

## Key Behavioral Patterns

- Higher activity users show stronger transaction volume and higher frequency.
- Weekend usage tends to increase for high-activity users, indicating flexibility in usage times.
- Payment and transfer-heavy users show more consistent activity compared to airtime-only users.

## What Defines High-Value Users

- High total transaction volume with consistent monthly activity.
- Balanced deposit and withdrawal flows (stable cash-in/cash-out behavior).
- Higher business usage signals (payments, merchant-like entities).

## Recommendations for Providers

1. Launch targeted rewards for high-frequency and high-volume users to improve retention.
2. Design business bundles for users with payment-heavy patterns.
3. Promote weekend promotions to amplify weekend usage for medium activity users.

## Notes on Ethics and Limitations

- Activity labels are data-driven and may shift with new data.
- Classification is descriptive and should not be used as sole input for credit decisions.
- Continued monitoring is needed to avoid unintended bias.
